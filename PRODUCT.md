# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Technical leaders, founders, and operations teams in Ecuador and LATAM who need a senior data engineer. They arrive from referrals, GitHub, or search with little time and need to understand scope, credibility, and how to start a conversation.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that sells Juan Palomino as a senior data engineer and entrepreneur: pipelines, cloud, analytics, and AI workflows that turn messy data into systems that support business decisions. Success is a clear positioning read in under 30 seconds and a low-friction path to contact.

## Brand Personality

Direct, technical, confident. Editorial brutality on type, not corporate softness. Feels like a studio identity for someone who ships infrastructure, not a generic SaaS landing.

## Anti-references

Cream-and-slate startup templates, identical icon-card grids, gradient hero metrics, glassmorphism cards, numbered section eyebrows on every block, and "transform your business" marketing filler.

## Design Principles

1. Type carries the brand before decoration does.
2. Show competence through specificity (stack, process, outcomes), not buzzwords.
3. Dark field, sharp accent: one lime signal, one warm orange for contrast moments.
4. Motion supports hierarchy; content is never hidden waiting for animation.
5. Contact is explicit: verb + object on every primary action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast on body copy. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (no scroll-gated visibility). Custom cursor is decorative only; system cursor remains on touch and reduced-motion paths.
